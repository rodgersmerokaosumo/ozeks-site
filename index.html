Features

🧭 AI Travel Chat

Conversational assistant to plan, explore, and discover with ease.

🗺️ Interactive Map

Leaflet/Mapbox to show points of interest, dining, and events.

📍 GIS Insights

View heatmaps for safety, schools, traffic, and local zones.

💰 Price Comparison

Fetch rates from Booking.com, Uber, Skyscanner, and more.

🧳 Smart Itinerary

Drag-and-drop schedules and auto-suggested routes.

🌿 Eco Filter

Discover and support sustainable travel options.

📸 Social Media Overlay

Pull TikTok and Instagram content tagged at attractions.

🏨 Corporate Planner

Search venues and generate retreat/conference plans.

🛰️ GPS Navigation

Get directions, trails, and scenic viewpoints instantly.

Powered by Modern Tech

Frontend: React, Next.js

Maps: Leaflet.js / Mapbox GL

AI: GPT-4 API, HuggingFace

Backend: Flask / Node.js

Database: PostgreSQL + PostGIS

Hosting: Vercel, AWS / GCP
